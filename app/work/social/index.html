Mirra

  • Brand & visual identity
  • Visual social strategy
  • Web design
  • Digital design
  • Photography
Lama Six provided Mirra with a visual identity, site, and Instagram presence that felt nothing like anything else in the beauty space. We pushed the boundaries of expected, frilly beauty visuals and illustrations to capture immediate attention and represent the radical ethos of this particularly inclusive community and founder. Mirra saw big growth its core newsletter and successfully closed a seed round following the creation of their consistent visual strategy.
